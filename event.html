About the event

The Mr. Oei NFT special event is one of a kind! This only takes place once a year, and it’s a convention where you can collaborate with your favourite NFT makers and share ideas and even create them! There will even be a 30-minute Q&A session where the star of the collection, Mr. Oei, will take any questions and answer them live on stage. Lastly, there will be an event where everyone can create a custom NFT. It will be judged by our professional NFT designer Jacky Tran.The winner will receive $1,000, plus their NFT will be added to the collection! This event is one week long, and all the dates are visible on the right.
