About Us

About our company and how each NFT is made.

Founded in 2021
By: Shankar Rohit.

How we started off as a company

Mr. Oei’s NFTs were initially a joke just for my amusement, and then we realized the real power and value of these tokens. My professional NFT designer Jacky Tran is the individual who has directed all of the images taken, and he has assisted me in the creation of this fantastic brand. He is also the owner of an anime site called The Only Anime Opinion , which you can find below.

How we make each NFT

We have a rigorous procedure for each NFT. First, we have to gather all the camera crew to take the picture, and then we get Mr. Oei , the main star of this collection. We have him do a pose and then take the picture. We send it off to our editing team, ensuring the quality of the NFT is up to par and editing the photo in case anything is wrong with it. This can be a lengthy process, but it is all designed to make sure we stay consistent, and the quality will be the highest it can be.

Our future plans

We have many plans for Oei's NFTs. Some of them include expanding the variety of NFTS and even making some into customizable gifts or videos. We plan to add many more variants to your most loved NFTS while still maintaining the premium quality and price point. We also plan to merge with some of our partnered brands such as The Only Anime Opinion, Frank & Oak and many more.

Our Staff Members

One of the most important members of the Oei NFT’s company is Mr. Oei himself. He is the star of all the NFTS, and he is what our brand is based around. We also have the NFT designer/image assistant Jacky Tran who leads the image-taking process. Lastly, our photo editors, Michael OwisandHenry Chung perfect the picture for the final cut.
